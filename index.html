digital medical center's patient portal


Project for an Argentine healthcare tech startup that offer services for patients to schedule appointments and a feature to watch and upload their own medical tests.

This feature allows patients to have more control over their healthcare information and to easily share it with their healthcare providers.

enayuno: fasting time tracker


I was working on a side project, which involved designing a mobile app that could help users track their fasting habits in a more efficient and effective manner. Additionally, the app would have a user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to input and view their fasting data at any time.

With this app, users would be able to achieve their fasting goals in a more organized and structured way, leading to a healthier and happier lifestyle overall.

medical institutions deals platform


Working as the only Product Designer and member of the frontend team in a healthcare startup SaaS for small to medium medical professionals, I was involved in the entire process of redesigning and refactoring of one the three most critical features of the product.

I researched, designed the solution, and developed the visual part of the code for a module that allows medical centers to set the methods for charging patients or insurance companies.

financial brand and site


This project involved creating an entirely new brand image, including a landing website, for a financial services company based in four cities in Argentina.

The main goals were to communicate the flexibility and serious service offered to professionals and small businesses. The project took three months to complete and is still in production more than two years later, with over 10,000 visitors.
